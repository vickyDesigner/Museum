Vase with Poppies

Year - 1886
Medium - Oil on canvas
Dimensions - 56.0 cm × 46.5 cm (22.0 in × 18.3 in)
Flowers were the subject of many of van Gogh's paintings in Paris, and one of his many interests due in great part to his regard for flowers. In Paris friends and acquaintances sent bouquets of flowers weekly for his still life paintings. He also purchased bouquets inexpensively, choosing flowers in a variety of colors for his paintings.
