Vincent Willem van Gogh

A Dutch post-impressionist painter who is among the most famous and influential figures in the history of Western art. In just over a decade, he created about 2,100 artworks, including around 860 oil paintings, most of which date from the last two years of his life. They include landscapes, still lifes, portraits and self-portraits are characterised by bold colours and it is dramatic, impulsive and expressive brushwork that contributed to the foundations of modern art.
